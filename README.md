Discover your own fashion taste.

Fashion Spectrum analyzes the images you intuitively like and reveals the underlying style tendencies behind them.

---

## Concept

What does your "favorite" really mean?

By selecting images you feel drawn to, this app:

- Visualizes your fashion orientation  
- Translates it into descriptive language  
- Quantifies it into measurable axes  

The goal is to make your sense of style visible and understandable.

---

## How it Works

The app leverages a machine learning model to extract feature embeddings from selected images and analyzes patterns across them.
